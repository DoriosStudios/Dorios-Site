The **Singularity Fabricator** is an extreme end-game duplicator that uses **Dark Matter** and very high DE requirements.

---

## Machine Information

| Setting | Value |
|----------|--------|
| **Energy Capacity** | 1,000,000,000 DE (1 GDE) |
| **Minimum Energy Cost** | 55,296,000,000 DE per craft |
| **Base Processing Rate** | 2,560,000 DE/t |
| **Fluid Tank Capacity** | 1,024,000 mB (Dark Matter) |
| **Minimum Processing Time** | 3,600s (1 hour) |
| **Upgrade Slots** | None |

## How It Works

1. Insert a compatible singularity template.
2. Fill the machine with **Dark Matter**.
3. Provide sustained high-tier DE throughput.
4. Wait for real-time completion and collect both outputs.

---

## Cost and Time Rules

- **Minimum craft time:** 3,600 seconds.
- **Minimum DE cost:** 55,296,000,000 DE.
- Additional scaling can increase total cost based on dynamic timing parameters.

---

## Compatible Templates (Current Base)

- Totem of Undying
- Nether Star
- Dragon Egg
- Wither Skeleton Skull
- Dragon Head

> All supported templates are processed under the singularity minimum-cost rules.
