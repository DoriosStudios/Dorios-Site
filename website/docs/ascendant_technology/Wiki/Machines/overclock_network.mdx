# Overclock Network

The **Overclock Network** is a dual-machine system where the **Overclock Relay** and **Overclock Tower** depend on each other to operate at full capacity.

| Titanium | 500 | 1.0 | 1.25× | +0.438 | -23.8% |

---

## How Relay + Tower Work Together

1. The **Tower** burns fuel and generates overclock level/effectiveness.
2. The **Relay** scans the network and receives the strongest valid overclock source.
3. The Relay applies boost to eligible machines and distributes up to **20,000 DE/t**.
4. Machines keep the boost while relay refresh remains active.

---

## Notes

- Generators are excluded from overclock boost.
- Network coverage is limited by scan radius and cable connectivity.
- For long sessions, Titanium provides the most stable burn duration.
