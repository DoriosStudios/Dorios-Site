## Practical notes

- This machine uses **Dark Matter** instead of **Liquified Aetherium**.
- It is meant for a small set of high-end templates.
- Long craft times and high costs make each run worth planning before you start.

## How It Works

1. Insert a compatible singularity template.
2. Fill the machine with **Dark Matter**.
3. Provide sustained high-tier DE throughput.
4. Wait for real-time completion and collect both outputs.

### Output behavior

- The machine returns the **original item**.
- It also creates **one additional copy**.
- Clear output space matters a lot because these crafts are too expensive to risk blocking at the finish line.

---

## Before you commit to a run

- Make sure the template is one of the supported singularity items.
- Fill the tank with enough **Dark Matter** before starting.
- Confirm your energy network can stay stable for the entire cycle.

---

## Cost and Time Rules

- **Minimum craft time:** 3,600 seconds.
- **Minimum DE cost:** 55,296,000,000 DE.
- Additional scaling can increase total cost based on dynamic timing parameters.

---

## Compatible Templates

- Totem of Undying
- Nether Star
- Dragon Egg
- Wither Skeleton Skull
- Dragon Head

> All supported templates are processed under the singularity minimum-cost rules.
