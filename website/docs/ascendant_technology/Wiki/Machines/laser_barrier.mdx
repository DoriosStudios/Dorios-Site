## How It Works

Laser Barrier projects a damaging field in its facing direction, with damage pulses consuming energy. Upgrades can increase size or reduce energy cost, but overclocking is ineffective. Sneaking players are immune to damage, making it ideal for base defense and area denial.

---

## Upgrades

- **Length (Size Upgrade):** Increases the length of the barrier projection.
- **Height (Size Upgrade):** Increases the height of the barrier projection.
- **Energy Upgrade:** Reduces per-cycle energy cost.
