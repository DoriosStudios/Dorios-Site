The **Laser Barrier** projects an energy wall in its facing direction and periodically damages entities in the active field.

---

## Machine Information

| Setting | Value |
|----------|--------|
| **Energy Capacity** | 320,000 DE |
| **Energy Consumption** | 800 DE per damage pulse cycle |
| **Field Size** | Configurable width and height |
| **Damage Pulse** | Periodic AoE damage in field range |
| **Upgrade Limit** | Up to +8 size, up to 8 energy levels |

## How It Works

1. Place the controller facing the area you want to protect.
2. Supply constant DE to keep the field active.
3. Add upgrades to increase dimensions or improve efficiency.
4. The field collapses automatically when power is lost.

---

## Upgrades

- **Length (Size Upgrade):** increases barrier length.
- **Height (Size Upgrade):** sneak while applying to increase height.
- **Energy Upgrade:** reduces per-cycle energy cost.

---

## Notes

- Sneaking players are not damaged by barrier pulses.
- Barrier effectiveness depends on facing and clear projection path.
