## How to obtain

- Crafted directly at a crafting table from **Titanium** using the normal armor patterns.
- Titanium is much easier to scale than Aetherium, which makes this set the more realistic mass-production option for normal progression.
- Repairs can use either **Titanium** or **Titanium Plate**, plus duplicate armor pieces when you want the larger repair jump.

## Why players use it

### Easy to build and maintain

Titanium Armor is the kind of set you can actually commit to during normal progression.
The material is much easier to scale than Aetherium, which makes this the more realistic choice for everyday survival and repeated repairs.

### Straightforward protection

This set does not rely on extra gimmicks. What you get is simple and useful:

- solid armor points
- respectable durability
- easy repair options

That makes Titanium a great "daily use" set before you move into rarer top-end gear.

### Friendly upkeep

The repair path is simple:

- **Titanium** gives smaller, easy repairs
- **Titanium Plate** gives a stronger repair
- a duplicate armor piece restores half durability

---

## Practical notes

- Titanium Armor is the sensible stepping stone before full Aetherium protection.
- If your resource flow supports plates consistently, maintaining the set is straightforward.
- It is the better choice when you want dependable gear without waiting for Ascendant's rarer late-game materials.
