---

## How to obtain

- The normal Titanium sword and mining tools are crafted directly from **Titanium** at a crafting table.
- The **Paxel** extends the line into hybrid utility by merging multiple tool roles into one item.
- The **AIOT** is crafted from the **Titanium Sword**, **Titanium Paxel**, **Titanium Hoe**, extra **Titanium**, **string**, and **sticks**.
- Repairs can use either **Titanium** or **Titanium Plate**, plus duplicate tools for the larger restore.

## What makes this set special

### The AIOT is the big convenience upgrade

The Titanium AIOT is where this line starts to feel truly special:

- it covers several tool roles in one item
- it tills land in a **3x3 area**
- while sneaking, it can make paths and clear snow in a **3x3 area**
- it can also help with automatic replanting when you harvest supported crops

It is the easiest way to get area-based farming and utility before moving into Aetherium.

### The Hammer adds better mining utility

The Titanium Hammer is meant for more than normal block breaking. On supported blocks, it can turn the regular drop into a more processed result,
which makes it a nice upgrade for players who want better mining utility early.

It also has extra pebble-gathering utility on dirt and grass-type blocks.

### The Paxel is the practical middle ground

The Titanium Paxel is perfect when you want to stop carrying separate mining tools all the time.
It handles the everyday pickaxe, axe, and shovel jobs without needing the full all-in-one power of the AIOT.

---

## Practical notes

- Titanium Tools are the best all-round utility set before Aetherium enters the picture.
- The AIOT is the easiest way to add area farming and path-making without committing to the Aetherium line yet.
- If your goal is mostly mining output conversion, the Hammer is the more specialized pickup.
