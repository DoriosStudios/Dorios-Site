## How to make Dark Matter

Dark Matter is produced in the [Liquifier](/docs/ascendant_technology/Wiki/Machines/liquifier), and it has several different input routes.

| Input | Output |
|---|---|
| **2 Ancient Debris** | **1000 mB Dark Matter** |
| **3 Void Essence** | **750 mB Dark Matter** |
| **2 Obsidian** | **500 mB Dark Matter** |
| **4 Stabilized Obsidian Dust** | **1000 mB Dark Matter** |
| **Compressed Obsidian** tiers | large bulk Dark Matter outputs |

This variety is important: Dark Matter is not tied to one single farm route. You can lean into Nether materials, End-adjacent residue work, or bulk compression depending on how your factory is set up.

---

## What Dark Matter is used for

### Catalyst Weaver refinement

Dark Matter is part of two notable native [Catalyst Weaver](/docs/ascendant_technology/Wiki/Machines/catalyst_weaver) recipes.

- **Aetherium Shard + Amethyst Shard + 800 mB Dark Matter → Refined Aetherium Shard**
- **Glass Bottle + Crushed Endstone + 100 mB Dark Matter → Void Essence**

That gives Dark Matter an important role in both shard upgrading and void-oriented material loops.

### Singularity Fabricator runtime

The [Singularity Fabricator](/docs/ascendant_technology/Wiki/Machines/singularity_fabricator) is built around **Dark Matter** as its default working fluid.

In other words, Dark Matter is not just a recipe ingredient here — it is part of the machine's core operating identity.

### Capsule logistics

Dark Matter is also available in capsule form, including larger storage steps and an infinite variant.

---

## Practical notes

- Dark Matter is one of the best examples of Ascendant's late-game philosophy: multiple expensive sources feeding a small set of very powerful systems.
- If you are already making **Stabilized Obsidian Dust**, turning part of that line into Dark Matter can be a clean factory loop.
- If you need very large amounts, the **compressed obsidian** route is the real bulk-production path.
