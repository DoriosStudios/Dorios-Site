## Current status

Right now, Tungsten is best understood as **announced and prepared**, not implemented.

- there is **no live Tungsten ore progression** in the current gameplay build
- there are **no active Tungsten machine recipes**
- there is **no current survival obtain method**
- the add-on already includes **prepared art assets** for multiple Tungsten forms

That means the line is clearly planned, but not yet part of the playable resource economy.

---

## Planned role in Ascendant Technology

Design notes and roadmap direction place Tungsten in a very specific niche.

- **heat-resistant internals** for hard-working industrial machines
- **dense machine casings** and durable structural parts
- a future **tool and armor** baseline with an emphasis on endurance rather than prestige
- potential **thermal upgrades** and safer sustained operation for heavier machinery

This positions Tungsten as a future bridge between standard industrial materials and the harsher thermal mechanics planned for Ascendant's superior-machine program.

## What to expect later

If Tungsten follows the direction already outlined for Ascendant Technology, it will likely become the material you use when a machine needs to:

- run hot for long periods
- handle thermal stress more safely
- support heavier industrial throughput
- stay durable under punishing production conditions

That makes it a very natural fit for the add-on's future thermal and superior-machine systems.

For now, Tungsten is a **forward-looking wiki entry**. Keep it in mind as planned progression, not current progression.
