## How to obtain Titanium

### Mine Deepslate Titanium Ore

Titanium starts in the world as **Deepslate Titanium Ore**.

- It generates in the Overworld between roughly **Y -48 and Y -32**.
- It is much more common than Aetherium, making it the more practical metal for large builds.
- Breaking the ore gives **Raw Titanium**, not finished ingots.

Without special fortune scaling, the base drop is modest; with better fortune, the raw yield climbs sharply.

### Sieve for Titanium Chunks

Titanium also has a machine-friendly alternative source.

- **Crushed Cobbled Deepslate** can yield **Titanium Chunks** at **10% chance** with **tier 4** requirements.
- Those chunks can then be crushed into **Raw Titanium**.

That makes Titanium one of the easiest Ascendant metals to automate once your crushing and sieving loops are online.

### Smelt or burn it into ingots

After that, refinement is straightforward.

- **Raw Titanium → Titanium Ingot**
- **Deepslate Titanium Ore → Titanium Ingot** in the [Industrial Burner](/docs/ascendant_technology/Wiki/Machines/industrial_burner)
- standard smelting recipes also exist for the ore line

Compared to Aetherium, Titanium follows a much more familiar ore-to-metal path.

### Press ingots into plates

Titanium becomes far more valuable once you start pressing it.

- **2 Titanium Ingots → 1 Titanium Plate**

That plate form is one of the most important machine-building materials in the add-on.

## What Titanium is used for

Titanium is everywhere in Ascendant's industrial tier.

- full **Titanium armor** and **tool** progression
- advanced tools such as the **Hammer**, **Paxel**, and **AIOT**
- **Titanium Mesh** and **Titanium Fishing Net**
- **Titanium Conveyor** variants for movement infrastructure
- **Titanium Plates** for a large number of advanced machine recipes
- **Overclock Relay** and several superior-machine craft paths

Titanium tools and gear can also be repaired with **Titanium Ingots** or **Titanium Plates**, which gives the plate form value both in construction and in maintenance.

- **Raw Titanium** — the main mined form from Titanium ore.
- **Titanium Ingot** — the standard refined metal for crafting.
- **Titanium Plate** — the machine-grade version used in many advanced block recipes.

- **Titanium Dust** — part of the resource family, though less central than raw, ingot, and plate forms in current progression.

## Practical notes

- If you are expanding machines, treat **Titanium Plate** as the form that matters most.
- If you are still building your resource network, **Titanium** is the safer metal to scale in bulk before pushing hard into Aetherium.
- Titanium is best understood as Ascendant's **industrial workhorse**: dependable, craft-heavy, and deeply tied to machine construction.
