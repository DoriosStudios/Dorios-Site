## Energy & Storage Capacities

| Tier | Rate (DE/t) | Energy Capacity |
|------|-------------|-----------------|
| **Basic** | 8 DE/t | 64,000 DE |
| **Advanced** | 32 DE/t | 256,000 DE |
| **Expert** | 128 DE/t | 1,024,000 DE |
| **Ultimate** | 800 DE/t | 6,400,000 DE |

---

## Behavior

The Wind Turbine produces energy continuously based on environmental wind conditions.  
It does not require sunlight, fuel, or player interaction to operate.

Energy generation may vary depending on placement and surrounding conditions, but stored energy is always retained when the block is broken, allowing safe relocation.

---

## Wind Turbine UI

The Wind Turbine interface displays its **current energy production rate** and **stored Dorios Energy (DE)**.  
The UI updates automatically based on environmental conditions and tier.
