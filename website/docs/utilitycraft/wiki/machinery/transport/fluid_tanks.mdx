## Tank Tiers

| Tier | Capacity |
|------|----------|
| **Basic Fluid Tank** | 8,000 mB |
| **Advanced Fluid Tank** | 32,000 mB |
| **Expert Fluid Tank** | 128,000 mB |
| **Ultimate Fluid Tank** | 512,000 mB |

---

## How They Work

Fluid Tanks are **passive storage blocks** — they don't consume energy or require any configuration.  
They store fluid internally using an invisible entity whose health represents the fluid level.

Tanks can be used with:

- **Fluid Pipes & Extractors** — to move fluids between machines.
- **XP Drain** — placed on top to drain player XP into the tank.
- **XP Spout** — placed on the side to release stored XP as orbs.
- **XP Condenser** — uses tanks to store and manage experience.

## How to Use

1. Place a **Fluid Tank** where you need fluid storage.  
2. Connect it to your fluid system using **Fluid Pipes** or attach an **XP Drain** on top / **XP Spout** on the side.  
3. The tank fills automatically when fluid is supplied. Each tier upgrades from the previous one in the crafting recipe.
