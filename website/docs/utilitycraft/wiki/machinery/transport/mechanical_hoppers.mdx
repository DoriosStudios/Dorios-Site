<p>
	UtilityCraft adds <b>four upgraded hoppers</b> that go beyond what vanilla hoppers can do — pulling, pushing, dropping, and vacuuming items with faster speeds and configurable filters.
</p>

---

## The Four Hoppers

| Block | What It Does |
|-------|-------------|
| **Mechanic Hopper** | Pulls items from above and outputs them to the side or below — like a vanilla hopper, but faster and filterable. |
| **Mechanic Upper** | The reverse hopper — pulls items from **below** and pushes them **up** or to the side. |
| **Mechanical Dropper** | Drops items into the world in the direction it faces. Great for feeding mobs or moving items into specific spots. |
| **Ender Hopper** | Vacuums up dropped items within a configurable radius. Perfect for mob farms and item collection. |

---

## How They Work

### Mechanic Hopper & Mechanic Upper

These work like an enhanced vanilla hopper:
- Place them adjacent to a container or machine.
- They automatically pull items from one side and output to the other.
- The **Mechanic Upper** reverses the direction — it pulls from below instead of above.

Both can optionally pull from **Minecarts** (Chest Minecarts and Hopper Minecarts) that are nearby, togglable in their settings.

### Mechanical Dropper

- Faces a direction when placed.
- Drops items from its inventory into the world in that direction.
- Useful for feeding items into specific areas or automated disposal.

### Ender Hopper

- Picks up all dropped items within a configurable **pickup radius**.
- Has an **ON/OFF toggle** and a **radius slider** in its settings.
- Ideal for mob grinders, crop farms, or any setup that produces scattered drops.

---

## Speed Upgrades

All four hoppers benefit from **Speed Upgrades**:

### Mechanic Hopper / Upper / Dropper

| Upgrade Level | Transfer Speed |
|---------------|---------------|
| 0 (No Upgrades) | Every 5 ticks |
| 1 | Every 4 ticks |
| 2 | Every 3 ticks |
| 3 | Every 2 ticks |
| 4 | Every tick |

### Ender Hopper

| Upgrade Level | Pickup Speed |
|---------------|-------------|
| 0 (No Upgrades) | Every 10 ticks |
| 1 | Every 8 ticks |
| 2 | Every 6 ticks |
| 3 | Every 4 ticks |
| 4 | Every 2 ticks |

---

## Filter Upgrades

Adding a **Filter Upgrade** to any mechanical hopper unlocks item filtering:

- Switch between **Whitelist** (only allow specific items) and **Blacklist** (block specific items) modes.
- Add or remove items from the filter list.
- Filters apply to all operations — pulling, outputting, and minecart interaction.
