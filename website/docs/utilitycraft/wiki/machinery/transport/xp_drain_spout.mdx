## XP Drain

The **XP Drain** sits on top of a **Fluid Tank** and drains XP from any player standing on it.

| Setting | Value |
|----------|--------|
| **Drain Rate** | 10 XP per tick |
| **Detection Radius** | 0.8 blocks |
| **Placement** | Top face of any Fluid Tank |
| **Energy Usage** | None |

### How It Works

1. Place a **Fluid Tank** (any tier).  
2. Place an **XP Drain** on top of the tank.  
3. Stand on the drain — your XP will automatically flow into the tank below.  
4. The drain stops when the tank is full or when you run out of XP.

:::info
The XP Drain can only be placed directly on top of a Fluid Tank. It will not attach to any other block.
:::

## XP Spout

The **XP Spout** attaches to the side of a **Fluid Tank** and releases stored XP as orbs when opened.

| Setting | Value |
|----------|--------|
| **Output Rate** | Up to 5 XP orbs per tick |
| **Placement** | Side face of any Fluid Tank |
| **Energy Usage** | None |
| **Toggle** | Left-click to open/close |

### How It Works

1. Place a **Fluid Tank** that contains XP fluid.  
2. Place an **XP Spout** on the side of the tank.  
3. **Left-click** the spout to open it — XP orbs will begin spawning from the spout.  
4. Click again to close it. When the tank empties completely, the spout stops automatically.

The spout releases orbs in the direction it faces, making it easy to aim XP into a collection area or directly at a player.

## Example Setup

> Place a **Fluid Tank** → put an **XP Drain** on top → stand on it to store XP.  
> Then attach an **XP Spout** on the side → click to release XP orbs when needed.

💡 **Tip:** Combine with a **Mob Grinder** + **XP Magnet** setup to automatically collect XP from mob farms, store it in tanks, and dispense it on demand.
