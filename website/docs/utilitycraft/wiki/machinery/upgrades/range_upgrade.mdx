## Where It Works

### Machine slot usage

- **Harvester** (in machine upgrade slots)

### Applied block usage

- **Fan**
- **Ender Hopper**
- **Mob Grinder**
- **Way Center**

---

## Practical Examples

- Harvester covers a larger crop area per cycle.
- Ender Hopper can collect drops from farther away.
- Fan can push entities through a longer path.
- Mob Grinder can operate over a larger selectable radius.
- Way Center can reach farther destinations.

---

## Harvester Area Growth

On Harvester, the effective side length follows:

- side = (range × 2) + 3

That means:

| Range Level | Harvest Area |
|---:|---:|
| 0 | 3x3 |
| 1 | 5x5 |
| 2 | 7x7 |
| 3 | 9x9 |
| 4 | 9x9 + Yield teleport |
