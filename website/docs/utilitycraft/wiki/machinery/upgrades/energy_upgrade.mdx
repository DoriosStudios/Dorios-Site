## Where It Works

Energy Upgrade is a **machine slot upgrade** used in most powered UtilityCraft machines that support upgrades.

It does not work as a direct right-click upgrade on transport blocks.

---

## Practical Effect

- Reduces effective energy consumption while the machine runs.
- Becomes even more important when stacking Speed Upgrades.

💡 Best combo for late game: **Speed Upgrade + Energy Upgrade**.

---

## Efficiency Notes

- Total Energy levels are capped at 8.
- Higher levels progressively reduce the real energy cost multiplier.
- The efficiency formula is applied dynamically by the machine core.

For players: just remember **more Energy Upgrades = cheaper operation per cycle**.
