## Where It Works

- **Mob Grinder** only

---

## Practical Effect

- Raises the damage ceiling that can be selected in the Mob Grinder UI.
- The actual damage used is still chosen by the player in settings.

So this upgrade does not auto-force max damage — it unlocks higher selectable levels.

---

## How to Apply

1. Hold a **Damage Upgrade**.
2. Use it (Sneak + Right-Click) on the Mob Grinder.
3. Open the Mob Grinder settings and choose your desired damage level.
