## Where It Works

- **Mechanical Spawner** only

---

## Practical Effect

Higher Quantity levels increase spawn attempts during each cycle:

| Quantity Level | Spawn Behavior |
|---:|---|
| 0 | Base behavior |
| 1 | Extra spawn attempts |
| 2 | More spawn attempts |
| 3 | High spawn attempts |
| 4 | High attempts + bonus chance |

At level 4, the spawner also gets an extra bonus chance on top of the normal attempts.

---

## How to Apply

1. Hold a **Quantity Upgrade**.
2. Apply it to a Mechanical Spawner.
3. Repeat up to the block's max level.
