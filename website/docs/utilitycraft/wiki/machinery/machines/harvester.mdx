      The <b>Harvester</b> automates crop and block harvesting in a configurable area.
      It consumes energy per block harvested, making it efficient for farms of any size — especially when combined with hoppers or transport systems for collection.
    </p>
  </div>

---

### Machine Information

| Setting | Value |
|----------|--------|
| **Energy Cap** | 32,000 DE |
| **Energy Cost** | 100 DE per block harvested |
| **Accepts Speed Upgrades** | Yes |
| **Overclockable** | No |

💡 **Tip:** With Range Upgrades, the Harvester can cover up to a **9x9** area.  
At **Range Level 4**, items are dropped **behind the machine** for easy collection with hoppers.

## Range Levels

| Range Level | Area Covered | Drop Behavior |
|--------------|---------------|----------------|
| 0 | 3x3 | Drops on the ground |
| 1 | 5x5 | Drops on the ground |
| 2 | 7x7 | Drops on the ground |
| 3 | 9x9 | Drops on the ground |
| 4 | 9x9 | Drops behind the machine |

---

## Harvest Speed

| Speed Level | Ticks per Operation |

| 0 | 1200 |
| 1 | 800 |
| 2 | 400 |
