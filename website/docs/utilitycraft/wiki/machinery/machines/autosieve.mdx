      The <b>Autosieve</b> is a machine that automatically sifts <b>gravel, sand, dirt</b> and other sieveable materials to extract valuable drops.  
      It requires <b>Dorios Energy (DE)</b> and a <b>mesh</b> to operate efficiently.
    </p>
    <p>
      The Autosieve can be upgraded with <b>Speed Upgrades</b> to reduce processing time, and its efficiency increases depending on the amount of energy upgrades installed.

### Compressed Variants

The Autosieve also accepts compressed variants from the same categories:

- Compressed Gravel
- Compressed Dirt
- Compressed Sand
- Compressed Crushed Netherrack
- Compressed Crushed Endstone
- Compressed Crushed Cobbled Deepslate

For these entries, the drop pool remains equivalent to the base material, but item amounts are scaled for compressed processing (typically **x9** per successful drop).

> Data source: `BP/scripts/config/recipes/sieve.js`.
