The **Auto Fisher** generates fishing loot automatically using Dorios Energy.

It requires:
- A valid **Fishing Net** in the Net slot
- Nearby **water** (or a Sink) around the machine

---

## Machine Information

| Setting | Value |
|---|---|
| Energy Cap | 512,000 DE |
| Energy Cost | 6,400 DE per completed process |
| Base Rate | 40 |
| Upgrade Slots | 2 (slots 4 and 5) |
| Net Slot | Slot 6 |
| Output Range | Slots 7–15 |

> Source: `BP/blocks/machinery/machines/autofisher.json`

---

## How It Works

1. Place a Fishing Net in slot 6.
2. Ensure there is water close to the machine.
3. Keep enough energy available.
4. The machine rolls loot from the internal Auto Fisher loot table.

The selected net controls important stats like:
- Speed
- Loot chance multiplier
- Amount multiplier
- Rolls per cycle
- Tier and luck (for higher-tier and better enchanted drops)

---

## Notes

- If the machine cannot run, it shows warnings such as `No Net Item`, `Need Water!`, `No Energy`, or `Output Full`.
- Loot comes from `BP/scripts/config/recipes/fisher.js`.
- Some drops require higher net tiers.
