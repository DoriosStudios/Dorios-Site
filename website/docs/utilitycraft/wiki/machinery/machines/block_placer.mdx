## Speed Levels & Operation Time

| Speed Level | Ticks per Operation | Time per Placement (Seconds) |
|--------------|--------------------|-------------------------------|
| 0 (No Upgrades) | 80 | 4 |
| 1 | 40 | 2 |
| 2 | 20 | 1 |
| 3 | 10 | 0.5 |
| 4 | 5 | 0.25 |

📌 With max Speed Upgrades, the Block Placer can place blocks every **0.25 seconds**.

---

### How It Works

- Consumes **800 DE per operation**.  
- Places the block **directly in front** of it.  
- Has a **manual UI** for inserting blocks.  
- Accepts items from **Hoppers**, **Mechanical Hoppers**, and similar transport systems.

---

### How to Use

1. Place the Block Placer facing the direction you want blocks to be placed.  
2. Insert blocks manually or through item transport systems.  
3. Ensure it has enough stored energy (**800 DE per operation**).  
4. *(Optional)* Add **Speed Upgrades** to increase placement speed.
