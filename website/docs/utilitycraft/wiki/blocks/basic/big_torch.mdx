## Why Use It

- Great for big rooms and work floors.
- Helps keep dark corners under control.
- More reliable for long-running bases than regular single-point lighting.

---

## Coverage Area

The Big Torch places projected `light_block` points around itself (not a full filled circle).

<BlockInfoGrid
	leftLabel="Coverage Metric"
	rightLabel="Value"
	items={[

		{ label: 'Farthest X/Z offset from torch', value: '14' },
		{ label: 'Vertical projection points', value: 'Up to y + 3' },
		{ label: 'Projected light level', value: '15' }
	]}
/>

This means your lighting stays more consistent in big or busy areas.

---

## Runtime Behavior

- Tracked when placed.
- Removed from tracking when broken.
- Projection is periodically refreshed in the background.
