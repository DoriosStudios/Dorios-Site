## Why Players Use It

- Great for big rooms and work floors.
- Helps keep dark corners under control.
- Good "place it and forget it" lighting for active bases.

---

## Lighting in Practice

- It emits normal light from the block itself.
- It also adds extra light points around the area.
- These extra points are refreshed automatically in the background.

---

## Runtime Behavior

- Tracked when placed.
- Removed from tracking when broken.
- Projection is periodically refreshed in the background.
