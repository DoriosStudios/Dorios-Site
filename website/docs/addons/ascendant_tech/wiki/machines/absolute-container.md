# Absolute Container

[![](/img/addons/ascendant/absolute_container.png)](/img/addons/ascendant/absolute_container.png)

A late-game singular vault that combines massive item storage with internal energy and fluid reserves in one block.

## What it does
- Stores up to 168 item slots in a 14×12 grid.
- Keeps internal energy and fluid tanks with 25.6M capacity each.
- Shows a dedicated HUD for energy and fluid (no extra labels).
- Provides automatic item transfer to adjacent blocks.
- Integrates with energy and fluid networks.

## How to use
1. Place the block and open its inventory.
2. Store items in the main grid.
3. Connect cables/pipes for energy and fluids if you want network integration.

## Container Capabilities
- **Energy Capacity**: 25,600,000 DE (25.6 MDE)
- **Fluid Capacity**: 25,600,000 mB (25,600 buckets)
- **Storage Capacity**: 168 item slots (14×12 grid)
- **Item Output**: Automatic transfer from last 9 slots
- **Network Integration**: Full support for energy and fluid networks
- **Upgrade Slots**: None

## Inputs and Outputs
- **Items**: The machine automatically pushes items from the last 9 slots of the grid to the block in its facing direction.
- **Fluids**: The internal tank transfers fluids to adjacent connections.
- **Energy**: Can receive and distribute energy through connected cables.

## Storage Features
- **Massive Capacity**: 168 slots provide exceptional storage density in a single block.
- **Multi-Resource**: Simultaneously stores items, energy, and fluids.
- **Automated Output**: Last 9 slots act as output buffer for automation.
- **Network Hub**: Can serve as a central storage and distribution point.

## Use Cases
- **Central Storage**: Late-game warehouse for massive item collections.
- **Energy Buffer**: Large-scale energy storage for networks.
- **Fluid Storage**: Multi-bucket fluid reservoir.
- **Automation Hub**: Item sorting and distribution with automatic output.
